How it works

Simple for shops.
Magical for customers.

No downloads. No app. Works on any phone. Save the link once — use it forever.

For Shopkeepers — Step 01
📋
Register your business
Takes 2 minutes. Enter your business name, category, and area. Get a QR code and a shareable link instantly.
For Shopkeepers — Step 02
🖨️
Print the poster
Print your unique QR poster and hang it at your entrance. Customers scan it to join your queue — no app needed.
For Shopkeepers — Step 03
✅
Call the next customer
One tap on your dashboard moves the queue forward. The customer gets notified. No shouting. No chaos.
For Customers — First Visit
📱
Scan once. Save the link.
Scan the shop's QR or tap a shared link. Enter your name, get your token number, and bookmark the page — that's your permanent link to that shop.
For Customers — Every Visit After
🔖
Open link. Get token. Come when called.
No scanning needed again. Open your saved link, tap "Get Token", see your live position, and come to the shop only when it's your turn. Zero waiting.
